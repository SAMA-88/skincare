Perfect Skincare Tips & Routine

Find the best routine for your skin type

For Oily Skin

Control excess oil and shine with these simple steps:

  • Cleanse with a gentle, oil-free face wash.
  • Use an oil-free moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated.
  • Exfoliate 2-3 times a week to remove excess oil and dead skin.
  • Apply a clay mask weekly to reduce shine.

For Dry Skin

Hydration is key to glowing, soft skin:

  • Use a hydrating, creamy cleanser to avoid stripping natural oils.
  • Apply a rich, moisture-locking cream immediately after washing.
  • Use a humidifier at night to add moisture to the air.
  • Exfoliate gently with a scrub or a chemical exfoliant once a week.

For Combination Skin

Balance is the key for this tricky skin type:

  • Use a mild cleanser that doesn’t strip moisture from your skin.
  • Moisturize dry areas with a hydrating cream and oily areas with a lightweight lotion.
  • Exfoliate 1-2 times per week, focusing on the T-zone.
  • Apply different masks on different areas of your face as needed (multi-masking).

For Sensitive Skin

Keep it simple and soothing to avoid irritation:

  • Use a fragrance-free, gentle cleanser.
  • Avoid harsh exfoliants and scrubs—use gentle alternatives like lactic acid.
  • Moisturize with a fragrance-free, hypoallergenic product.
  • Always patch-test new products before applying them to your face.
